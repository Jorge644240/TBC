About Bletchley

History


Founded in 2021, The Bletchley Corporation was first conceived due to the COVID-19 outbreak in 2020 to better the communication between the people of Colombia and the country's govenment.



Seeing the issue solved shortly after, Bletchley turned to web Development; Front End first, then Back End (and also Full Stack Development), so giving birth to our Web Development service.




Later, Bletchley created the App Development service through cross-platform development with Google Flutter and Dart, and eventually moved on to native app development for Android with Java and iOS with Swift.



Mission

Insofar Bletchley's existence has been shaped by the world around it, the Corporation's mission has always stayed the same.



We are bound to provide high-quality technological solutions regarding information technologies, software and hardware development, engineering and R+D (Research and Development), programming, and all-around Computer Science (CS) such that it is accessible to anyone, anytime, anywhere.

Vision

In years

Be recognised as a technological solutions provider and manufacturer in Colombia with the highest industry standards.

In years

Begin emerging into the Latin American technology market both virtually and physically with offices and outreach campaigns across the continent.

In years

Have the horizon set on global markets and goals pointing towards Silicon Valley, California.
